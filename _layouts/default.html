Research

My research concentrate on high-quality object insertion and lighting estimation, leveraging diffusion models to achieve seamless, realistic object compositing across diverse scenes. My goal is to develop compositing methods that integrate precise shading and shadow with user inputs, and accurate environmental conditions while minimizing the need for extensive paired data or supervised training.
